Welcome to Myextrasheet.com

Myextrasheet is an online storage facility that allows you to easily access your learning materials, documents, project works e.t.c and also connects you to relevant people in your Circle(network).

You can use myextrasheet to :

  1. Store materials, documents, project works that might be useful to your circle e.g Past questions
  2. Search for materials you might need.
  3. Share materials with your colleagues
  4. Retrieve missed or lost materials, documents, project works e.t.c
  5. Review concepts that you might have forgotten.
